4C Extra Fancy Nuts (Dry roast in oven at 350F for 7min then cooled)
1-1/2C Sugar
1/2C Corn Syrup
1/4t Cream of Tart
1/2C Water
2t Vanilla
2t Salt (large grain)

Add sugar, corn syrup, cream of tart, and water to pan and begin to heat on medium.
Mix well untill moist and then do not stir any more.
After mixture uncrystalizes wash remaining crystals from side of pan with water moisened pastry brush
Heat to xxxF
Add vanilla and swirle in
Pour mixtue into bowl of nuts
Imidelty scoop out onto cookie sheet with parchment paper and spread out
Sprinkle with salt
Cool then break apart
Store in air tight container
